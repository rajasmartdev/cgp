Our Specialized Services

Jaw Asymmetry Correction

Correct misaligned jaws caused by congenital malformations, disproportionate development, or trauma. Orthognathic surgery restores facial balance and perfects your bite for both function and aesthetics.

TMJ Disorders

Treatment for complex TMJ disorders causing jaw pain, clicking, locking, and chewing difficulties. From conservative therapy to surgical correction and complete joint replacement.

Pediatric Maxillofacial Surgery

Specialized care for children's facial conditions including birth deformities, trauma, and airway disruptions. Treatment designed to work with growing facial anatomy without hampering future development.

Facial & Cranio-maxillofacial Trauma

Expert reconstruction for accident-related injuries—from mild to severe life-threatening trauma. We treat fractures, soft tissue injuries, nerve damage, and correct improperly healed defects.

Microvascular Reconstructive Surgery

Advanced tissue transfer for facial reconstruction post-cancer treatment. Free flap surgery restores natural shape and appearance using your own tissue for excellent cosmetic results.

Congenital Anomalies Correction

Comprehensive treatment for birth defects including cleft lip/palate, hemifacial microsomia, and complex syndromes. Emergency interventions available for newborns requiring immediate attention.
