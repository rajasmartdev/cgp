Types of Braces We Offer

Metal Braces

Most effective and reliable option for all dental corrections. Durable, cost-effective solution for simple to complex cases.

Ceramic Braces

Tooth-colored brackets that blend naturally. Aesthetic appeal with the same effectiveness as metal braces.

Self-Ligating Braces

Advanced system with fewer adjustments and faster treatment time. More comfortable with quicker results.

Lingual Braces

Hidden behind teeth for complete invisibility. Perfect for those seeking maximum discretion during treatment.

Clear Aligners

Removable, transparent trays for mild to moderate corrections. Ideal for adults preferring discreet treatment.
